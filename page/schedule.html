12-Month Festival Schedule

  • Tagu / တန်ခူး (Mid-April)
    Thingyan Water Festival — Nationwide, especially grand in Yangon, Mandalay, Nay Pyi Taw
  • Kason / ကဆုန် (Mid-May)
    Bodhi Tree Watering Festival — Shwedagon Pagoda, local pagodas nationwide
  • Nayon / နယုန် (Mid-June)
    Start of Buddhist teaching season — Monasteries across Myanmar
  • Waso / ဝါဆို (Mid-July)
    Beginning of Buddhist Lent — Major pagodas and monasteries
  • Wagaung / ဝါခေါင် (Mid-August)
    Maha Dok Offering Festival — Mandalay and Sagaing monasteries
  • Tawthalin / တော်သလင်း (Mid-September)
    Boat Racing Festivals — Inle Lake, Ayeyarwaddy River towns
  • Thadingyut / သီတင်းကျွတ် (Mid-October)
    Festival of Lights — Nationwide, especially Yangon, Bagan, Taunggyi
  • Tazaungmon / တန်ဆောင်မုန်း (Mid-November)
    Robe Offering & Fire Balloon — Taunggyi and monasteries nationwide
  • Nadaw / နတ်တော် (Mid-December)
    Nat Worship and Literature Festivals — Mount Popa, universities
  • Pyatho / ပုသ်ဓမ္မ (Mid-January)
    Equestrian Festivals and Royal Ceremonies — Nay Pyi Taw, Mandalay
  • Tabodwe / တပေါင်း (Mid-February)
    Htamane Sticky Rice Festival — Monasteries and homes
  • Tabaung / တပို့တွဲ (Mid-March)
    Pagoda Festivals — Shwedagon, Shwesettaw, Shwemyethman
